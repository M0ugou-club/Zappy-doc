GUI

The GUI is a 3D graphical interface for the game that show the map and all the players in real time.
It show all the items on the map, the players, it shows an animation when a player is doing a ritual and when a player is broadcasting.

Controls

The GUI is controlled with the mouse and the keyboard.
The camera is always looking at the center of the map and is rotating around it.
The camera can be moved with ZQSD.
  • Z - Move the camera forward
  • Q - Move the camera to the left
  • S - Move the camera backward
  • D - Move the camera to the right
This also works with WASD or other keyboard layouts.
